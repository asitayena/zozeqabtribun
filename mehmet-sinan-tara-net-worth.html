What is Mehmet Sinan Tara's Net Worth?

Mehmet Sinan Tara is a Turkish businessman who has a net worth of $1.6 billion. He is the chairman of one of Turkey's largest construction companies, ENKA, which was founded by his father. Mehmet Sinan Tara already held a degree in civil engineering when he joined his father's company as a field engineer in 1980.

Next, he served as a general manager and eventually took over the entire company and became a board member. Under his helm, the company grew and now does contracting, construction, energy production, real estate investment and management. While being especially active in Russia, the company has also built U.S. embassy buildings in the Dominican Republic, Equatorial Guinea and Afghanistan.
