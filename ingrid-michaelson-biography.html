Ingrid Michaelson Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 19, 2024

Who is Ingrid Michaelson?

Ingrid Michaelson is an American singer and songwriter. The public noticed her for her albums like Everybody, Lights Out, and more.

Ingrid is an expert in Indie pop, Indie folk, Folk-pop, Alternative/Indie genres. In addition, is also an actress and was featured in Humor Me, The Muppets, etc.

Recently in April 2021, she teamed up with Zyan Malik and released a much-awaited music video titled To Begin Again.

Ingrid Michaelson- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born Ingrid Ellen Egbert Michaelson on 08 Dec 1979 in New York, United States. The name of her father is Carl Michaelson and her mother’s name is Elizabeth Egbert.

Her dad is a Music composer and her mom is a sculptor. Ingrid’s ancestry is Swedish-Dutch and her brother is Charles Michaelson.

In which school did Ingrid Michaelson attend?

Talking about her academics, she attended the Staten Island Technical High School and Binghamton University. From the latter, she received a degree in theater.

Also, she took classes in music at Third Street Music School and Dorothy Delson Kuhn Music Institute.

Ingrid Michaelson- Professional Life, Career

Ingrid Michaelson being a singer is also an actress. She released her first album in 2005 titled Slow the Rain. Her second album Girls and Boys came out after a year.

Then in 2008, 2009, and 2012, she gave her listeners three musical projects. They are Be OK, Everybody, and Human Again respectively. Her other albums are Lights Out, It Doesn’t Have to Make Sense, Songs for the Season, and Stranger Songs.

Ingrid’s hit singles are Girls Chase Boys, You and I, Over You, Missing Yiu, Winter Song, The Chain, etc. Besides her voice, she is also a great actress.

Her debut screen presence was with The Muppets in 2016. She was also seen in Humor Me as Allison, E-Bowla as Butterface, and Tigers and Young Men as Carol.
