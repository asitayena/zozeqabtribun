Daddy G Overview and Wiki

Early Years and Formation of Massive Attack

Grantley Evan Marshall, known popularly as Daddy G, was born on December 18, 1959, in Bristol, England, to West Indian parents. Growing up in a city known for its rich musical scene, Daddy G quickly became entrenched in the Bristol music scene.

His early involvement with the sound system group The Wild Bunch was pivotal. This group also included other notable members like Robert del Naja and Andrew Vowles, who would later co-found Massive Attack with him.

Pioneering the Bristol Sound

In 1988, following the disbandment of The Wild Bunch, Daddy G, along with Del Naja and Vowles, formed Massive Attack. This group became synonymous with the trip-hop genre, significantly influencing the Bristol Sound alongside artists like Portishead and Tricky.
