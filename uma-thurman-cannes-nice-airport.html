Uma Thurman and baby Luna jet out of Cannes

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 18, 2024

    Uma Thurman is spending some post-Cannes family time with her baby daughter Luna. The actress, who was seen at the film festival with financier partner Arpad Busson, travelled through Nice airport with the adorable youngster, cradling her in a baby carrier during the rare public outing. The star gently stroked her little girl's face as the pair waited to catch a flight back to New York.

    Dressed in slim fit jeans, gladiator sandals and a black cardigan, Uma kept in simple and chic for her long-haul journey home. Two days previously she stole the show in a custom-made Atelier Versace cream gown as she said goodbye to Cannes in style. Stepping out on Arpad's arm, Uma was seen gazing into her partner's eyes and sharing a tender kiss with him as they hit the red carpet at the premiere. Fashionista Olivia Palermo was also spotted making her way through the departure terminal on Tuesday to catch a flight home. The former MTV star, who looked super-stylish in nude flats, an A-line red, cream and black skirt, black t-shirt and studded necklace, also made a splash in Cannes.

    She put her best fashion foot forward at amfAR Cinema Against AIDS 2013 charity event on Thursday evening, wearing a daring floor length leopard-print Cavalli gown with a thigh-high split. The star sat next to Kylie Minogue at the event, which raised a staggering $32.3million for charity. Veteran Spice Girl Mel B looked pleased to be returning to British soil as she strutted through the airport in an all-black two piece with matching gold and black sandals. Writing on her blog before the film festival, Mel announced that her trip home was going to be a special one. "Heading to Cannes #Cinemoi and UK to see my Spices, yippeee!" said the star. Although Victoria Beckham is currently enjoying LA’s sunshine with the Beckham clan, the rest of the girls will be eagerly anticipating the Leeds-born singer’s arrival.
