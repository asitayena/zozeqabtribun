Wiki-Bio, Net Worth, Height, Career, Relationship.

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 23, 2024

Paige DeSorbo may be the youngest member in the Summer House, but she is not afraid to speak her mind. The fiery Italian-American is not afraid of sharing her opinions and is not afraid to stir up things. And while Paige is the youngest of the group, she is not too far in terms of success. The socialite is an entrepreneur who doubles down as a freelance television host and the ultimate fashionista. Paige is huge on Instagram and regularly updates her fans on several fashion tips. In this article, we will take a closer look at the wiki-bio of Paige DeSorbo.

Wiki/Bio Facts

Early Life

Paige DeSorbo was born on the 6th of November 1992. Born to Italian parents, Paige was born and raised in the USA. A talented child, Paige attended a catholic school when she was young and later moved to Holy Names High School. She decided to continue her higher education and decided to move to Saint Rose College. She graduated from college with a degree in journalism and decided to move to New York to pursue a career in modeling. However, things did not work out well for Paige and her parents offered to pay her rent until the foreseeable future.

Life as an influencer

While still at college, Paige started working at the CBS 6 in Niskayuna but later started working as an Instagram influencer. She began working on an entertainment news segment on her Instagram page, called Front “Paige” News. Talking about the segment, Paige revealed that there is no road map for what she was doing at the moment. While Paige is known for her fashion advice, the most sought after segment on her Instagram page is her ‘Looks for Less’ posts. The segment focuses on getting celebrity looks on a budget.

“We lived in Albany so I wasn’t running to Bergdorfs or something, so we would go to Forever 21 or Marshalls and find something that looked similar. I just put it on Instagram and people lost their minds over it.”

Paige began modeling from a young age and has appeared in the covers of several fashion magazines including Teen Vogue and was even seen on a series of teen fiction book covers.

Professional Career

Paige DeSorbo has had an impressive professional career over the time, both on and off Summer House. She started as a journalist and freelance TV host, who had worked as an executive assistant for ABC and a fashion writer for Betches Media. She also had a series on Instagram called Front Paige News, where she shared her fashion tips and pop culture insights. She joined the cast of Summer House in season three and became one of the most popular members of the show, thanks to her witty personality and stylish outfits. She also developed a close friendship with Hannah Berner, with whom she started a podcast called Giggly Squad in 2020. She continued to work as a TV host for Amazon Live, where she showcased her fashion advice and style tips. She also grew her Instagram following to over 926k followers, where she posted her outfits of the day and links to affordable stores where her fans could shop for similar looks. She is currently one of the most successful and influential members of the Summer House cast.

Summer House

Of all the cast members of Summer House, Paige is the one who has most benefited from her fame that came along with her series. The reality star revealed that the number of followers on her Instagram page has grown exponentially.

“It was the craziest feeling. I started the season with 3,000 followers then the show aired and I was growing 10,000 followers a week, but it’s Paige’s posts that keep fans coming.”

Personal Relationships

Paige has had an interesting journey when it comes to her personal relationships on and off Summer House. She joined the show in season three and had an on-and-off fling with Carl Radke, but they eventually decided to remain friends. She then started dating Perry Rahbar, a business owner who did not want to be on the show. She faced some conflict with her castmates who questioned her relationship and felt left out of her life. She also revealed that she and Perry broke up before season five.

She then met Craig Conover, a star of Southern Charm. They hit it off and started a long-distance relationship, as Craig lives in Charleston and Paige lives in New York. They made their relationship official on season five of Summer House, where they shared some romantic moments and also some challenges. They decided to move in together by the end of the season.

Paige and Craig are still going strong as of 2023. They have also appeared on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen and gushed about each other. Paige has said that Craig is “the best boyfriend ever” and that she is “very happy” with him.

Net Worth

Paige has an estimated net worth of $1 million. She makes money from appearing on Summer House, working for ABC and Betches Media, hosting Amazon Live and Giggly Squad, and influencing on Instagram.
