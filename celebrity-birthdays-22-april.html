Celebrity birthdays 22 April: Amber Heard, Jack Nicholson and Jeffrey Dean Morgan

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

A very happy birthday to Hollywood stars Amber Heard, Jack Nicholson and Jeffrey Dean Morgan. The trio are all celebrating their birthdays on 22 April, and to mark the occasion, we've taken a look at what they have been up to recently…

Amber Heard turns 32

Amber will no doubt be celebrating her special day in style! The Rum Diary actress will turn 32 on 22 April – one month before the two-year anniversary of her split from Johnny Depp. Amber, who filed for divorce from Johnny on 23 May 2016, was subsequently romantically linked to Elon Musk, but is now thought to be single. Work-wise, Amber is set to appear in two productions in 2018; upcoming thriller London Fields, and superhero film Aquaman, alongside Jason Momoa.

Jack Nicholson turns 81

Hollywood icon Jack will perhaps spend the day celebrating with his five children. The One Flew Over The Cuckoo's Nest actor has kept a low-profile in recent years – his most recent film was 2010's How Do You Know – and will no doubt relish some time with his loved ones. Speaking previously about his family, Jack once said: "Children give your life a resonance that it can't have without them… As a father I'm there all the time. I give unconditional love. And I have a lot of skills in terms of getting them to express themselves."

Jeffrey Dean Morgan turns 52

It will also be a family day for The Walking Dead star, who became a father for the second time on 6 March 2018. Jeffrey and his wife Hilarie Burton are the proud parents to son Gus, born 10 March, 2010, and this year announced the arrival of their baby daughter, George Virginia. Jeffrey has been on the promotional trail in recent weeks with his latest film, Rampage, alongside Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson, and Naomi Harris.
