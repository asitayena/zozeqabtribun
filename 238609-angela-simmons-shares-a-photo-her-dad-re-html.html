Angela Simmons Shares a Photo of Her Dad Rev Run &amp; Look-Alike Son Sutton on the Rapper's B-Day

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

In the spirit of celebrating Rev Run on his birthday, his daughter, Angela Simmons, flaunted an adorable picture of the star and her son, Sutton, who is the spitting image of his grandfather.

Angela Simmons took to Instagram to show off a captured moment of her father, Joseph Simmons, popularly known as Rev Run, and her son, Sutton, as they smiled gleefully in each other's company. The post was captioned:

"✨✨Happy birthday to the Best Dad in the World!!!! Love you Dad???✨."

Angela Simmons at WE tv's celebration of "Growing Up Hip Hop" Season 3 on July 20, 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

Rev Run wore a black sweater as he hugged his grandson affectionately. Sutton, who was on a red cardigan with a black collar, basked in the moment while holding onto his nose mask.

With a blurry background filled with fully grown pasture, the lovely duo seemed to be in high spirits as they posed for the headshot.
