Amber Isabella Williams Missing

Authorities have disclosed details about the red 2004 Ford F-150 with the Texas license plate BE88718 that Jennifer Carmony may be driving.  The truck might, however, have received a black spray paint job.

The El Paso Police Department at 915-212-4040 is asking anyone with information about the whereabouts of these missing youngsters or Jennifer Carmony to get in touch right now.
