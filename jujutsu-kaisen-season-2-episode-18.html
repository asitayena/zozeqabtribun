Spoiler Alert

Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 Episode 18 will adapt chapters 123 and 124 of the manga, which are titled “Right and Wrong” and “The Shibuya Incident, Part 50”. The episode will show the aftermath of the Shibuya Incident, which resulted in the death of many sorcerers and civilians, as well as the destruction of the city. The episode will focus on the confrontation between Nanami and Mahito, who encounter each other in the subway.

Nanami, who is severely wounded and exhausted, decides to fight Mahito one last time, knowing that he has no chance of survival. He recalls his past and his friendship with Yu Haibara, who died in a previous mission. He also thinks about Megumi, who he considers his successor.

As he prepares to face Mahito, he sees Yuji arriving at the scene. Yuji, who is already traumatized by the carnage caused by Sukuna, witnesses Nanami getting blown up by Mahito’s Idle Transfiguration. Nanami’s last words to Yuji are “You’ve got it from here”. Yuji, filled with anger and grief, attacks Mahito, who mocks him and reminds him of Junpei. Nobara, who refused to leave Shibuya, also joins the fight against Mahito. The episode will end with a cliffhanger, as Mahito reveals his new form, which resembles a human.

Recap of Previous Episodes

Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 Episode 17 was titled “Thunderclap, Part 2”. The episode showed the epic battle between Sukuna and Mahoraga, the strongest shikigami that Megumi summoned. Sukuna, who took over Yuji’s body for 30 minutes, decided to save Megumi from Mahoraga, as he had plans for him. Sukuna and Mahoraga fought across Shibuya, using their cursed techniques and physical abilities.

Sukuna realized that Mahoraga had the innate ability to adapt to any situation and counter any attack. Sukuna then used his domain expansion, Malevolent Shrine, which did not create a barrier, but instead increased the range and power of his attacks. He used his techniques, Fillet and Dissect, to target everything within a 200-meter radius, based on their cursed energy.

He managed to defeat Mahoraga with a flaming arrow and then delivered Megumi’s body to Shoko, the Jujutsu High doctor. He then returned to the spot where he fought Mahoraga, and gave back Yuji’s body. Yuji, who regained his consciousness, was horrified by the sight of the destroyed Shibuya and the corpses of the people. He blamed himself for the deaths and wished for his own death. The episode ended with Nanami, who was still alive but injured, walking in the subway.

Plotlines

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime series that follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the host of Sukuna, a powerful and evil curse. Yuji joins Jujutsu High, a school that trains sorcerers to exorcise curses, and teams up with Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, under the guidance of Satoru Gojo, the strongest sorcerer. Together, they face various threats from curses and cursed users, who seek to unleash Sukuna and plunge the world into chaos.
