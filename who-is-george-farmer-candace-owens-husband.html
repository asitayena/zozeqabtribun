George Thomas Farmer was Born and Raised in London

George Thomas was born in 1989 in London, United Kingdom. Until the age of 18, he had spent his whole life in London. His specific date of birth is unknown, but going by his year of birth, he is in the same age bracket as his wife, Candace.

Who is George Farmer’s Father?

George Farmer’s father is Lord Michael Farmer, a British businessman, former treasurer of the Conservative Party, and philanthropist. He is a well-known self-made commodities tycoon and former Tory party treasurer. He grew up in the slums of London and worked as an account clerk to cater for his family.

Through his hard work, he provided a solid start in life for his family from his skills as a commodities dealer. Consequently, George was raised in a politically active household with a diverse set of ideas and beliefs. As a result of his upbringing, he developed an early interest in politics.

Some reports also have it that he earned a degree in business management from Oxford. While in school, he was a member of numerous political organizations and was also quite active in them.

He Grew Up To Become An Astute Businessman Like His Father

George worked for an investment bank in London for eighteen months after graduating from Oxford University. However, since 2012, he has been trading base metals alongside his father in the commodity business, and they have been remarkably successful in doing so. His firm “Red Kite Group” is in charge of the copper market through which he has made millions in the metal industry.

They expanded the company into a hedge fund business as it progressed, focusing on mine investments and finance. Though George is often taken to be the main owner of the business, it is not entirely his as he founded the company with a group of partners. Obviously, he/they assembled the best heads in the industry to run the company as it has been remarkably successful.

Apart from his businesses in metal mining and finance, George Farmer has also held leadership positions in many other organizations. Some of these include his time as the chairman of Turning Point UK and when he was a member of the Bullingdon Club, an elite all-male dining club. Currently, Farmer is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of the social media app Parler. He was announced as the new CEO and successor of Mark Meckler. Before his new appointment, Geroge served as Parler’s Operating Chief and equally has a wealth of over 10 years of experience in the finance sector.
