Early Life

Steven John Carell was born on August 16, 1962, in Concord, Massachusetts, to his parents Harriet Theresa and Edwin A. Carell. He grew up in Acton, Massachusetts.

He went to several schools, including the Nashoba Brooks School. In 1984, he earned his bachelor’s degree in history from Granville, Ohio’s Denison University. As a student, he joined the Burpee’s Seedy Theatrical Company.

Personal Life

In 1995, Steve Carell got married to Nancy Walls. The couple has two children named Elisabeth and John born in 2001 and 2004 respectively. He grew up as a Roman Catholic. He was inspired to acting by Steve Martin, George Carlin, Bill Cosby, Peter Sellers, and John Cleese.
