Career

Offset belongs to a middle-class family, so making a career at international level was surely nail-biting for him. But as he was passionate about hip-hop music since childhood, he didn’t lose the hope and started making efforts for his dreams.

In 2009, he came together with two other boys, who both belonged to the same town and had the same upbringings as offset, and formed a hip-hop music group ‘Polo Club’ which was later renamed to ‘Migos’ in 2010. In the initial years, he didn’t have smooth sailing, but as time passed, people started recognising his talents.

After changing the name of the group in 2010, he released the first group tape in 2012, but it didn’t grab much attention. He and his group got the initial recognition in 2013 when the single song ‘Versace’ was released.

It was loved by the audience and has even made a place on Billboards list. But his career took a leaf in 2014 when the second mixtape titled ‘No Label 2’ was released. For this tape, Offset was particularly praised. Then, in 2015, Migos released its first album that went on becoming the number 3 on the list of the ‘Top Rap Albums Charts’. With this group album, Offset tasted the mainstream of success.

Just two years after the groundbreaking success of his debut studio album, Offset released second group album named ‘Culture’ in 2017 which reached even at number 1 spot on Billboard list.

Other than the singing, Offset also appeared in an episode of T series Atlanta.

Awards & achievements

Offset has given all the songs with his team members for their group Migos, so individually, he hasn’t won any awards. But his group Migos has won several nominations and awards including the BET Award.
