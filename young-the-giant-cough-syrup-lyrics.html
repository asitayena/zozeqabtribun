Young the Giant Cough Syrup Lyrics

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

“Cough Syrup Lyrics” is a song by the American rock band Young the Giant. It was released as the second single from their debut self-titled album in 2011. The song was written by the band’s lead vocalist Sameer Gadhia and explores themes of escapism and finding oneself.

About

Young the Giant

Young the Giant is an American rock band that was formed in Irvine, California, in 2004. The band consists of Sameer Gadhia (lead vocals), Jacob Tilley (guitar), Eric Cannata (guitar), Payam Doostzadeh (bass guitar), and Francois Comtois (drums). Originally known as The Jakes, the band changed their name to Young the Giant in 2010, just before the release of their debut album.

Young the Giant’s music is characterized by a blend of indie rock, alternative rock, and pop. They have released four studio albums to date: “Young the Giant” (2010), “Mind Over Matter” (2014), “Home of the Strange” (2016), and “Mirror Master” (2018). Their songs have received critical acclaim and commercial success, with chart-topping hits like “Cough Syrup,” “My Body,” and “Superposition.”

The band has also gained a reputation for their dynamic live performances, having toured extensively in the United States and internationally over the past decade. They have played at major music festivals such as Coachella, Lollapalooza, Bonnaroo, and Austin City Limits, among others.

FAQs

Why did Young the Giant change their name?

The band changed their name from The Jakes to Young the Giant in January 2010.

Lead singer Sameer Gadhia said the new name did not have any specific meaning, but that it epitomized the band they had become.

“I was thinking about what we want to portray,” Gadhia said. “We are still youthful, but when we aren’t, we still want to have that exuberance in our music.”

Is Young the Giant good in concert?

Yes, Young the Giant is known for their energetic and engaging live shows.

They have been praised for their tight musicianship, powerful vocals, and dynamic stage presence.

Fans often rave about the band’s ability to connect with the audience and create a truly memorable experience.

What is Young the Giant’s biggest hit?

Young the Giant’s biggest hit is “My Body.”

The song was released in 2011 as the lead single from their debut album, Young the Giant.

It has been certified triple platinum by the RIAA and has over 200 million streams on Spotify.
