Gregory Allan Lazzarato Wiki Biography

Gregory Allan Lazzarato was born on 20 April 1992, in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, of part-French, Lebanese and Italian descent, but is now known under the name Gigi Gorgeous or Giselle Loren Lazzarato, after revealing that she is transgender. She is an actress, internet personality, and model, best known for her work in various television series. All of her endeavors have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Gigi Gorgeous? As of late-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $2 million, mostly earned through success in her various endeavors, including being the subject of the award-winning documentary entitled “This is Everything: Gigi Gorgeous”. As she continues her career, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Gigi Gorgeous Net Worth $2 million

Gigi attended Iona Catholic Secondary School, and after matriculating enrolled at George Town College to study fashion, but later left the school to focus on her YouTube career. Meantime she was also a nationally-ranked diving champion. She began operating her YouTube channel in 2008, which started off as a makeup tutorial channel, and during this time she identified as a gay male. Later on, she started to focus more on fashion, lifestyle and vlogs, so more opportunities opened up for her, increasing her net worth.

In 2011, Gorgeous found her first acting work in the YouTube-based reality series entitled “The Avenue”, which would run for a total of two years. Afterwards, she started appearing more in her transgender form, featuring in “E! Celebrity Style Story” and “Project Runway: All Stars” – her net worth was rising steadily.

In 2015, she then appeared in the debut short film of YouTuber Shane Dawson, entitled “I Hate Myselfie”, and then reprised her role in the sequel. Later in the year, she collaborated with Miley Cyrus for various projects including appearing at the “2015 MTV Video Music Awards”. She has been featured in magazines such as “Marie Claire”, “People” and “Entertainment Tonight”, having been active in cosmetic events too, and has also modeled for Marco Marco’.

In 2016, Gigi had an incident in Dubai where she was denied entry due to her transgender status, as the UAE government doesn’t recognize her gender as legitimate; she was released after being detained for five hours. The following year, her documentary entitled “This is Everything: Gigi Gorgeous” was released through the Sundance Film Festival, and concurrently she was also named as one of the most influential people on the Internet by “Time” magazine. All of these endeavors have helped increase her net worth significantly.

For her personal life, it is known that Gorgeous once identified as a bisexual, but came out as a lesbian in 2016. She is presently dating heiress Natalia Williams from the Getty family. Some of the procedures she’s undergone include breast augmentation, rhinoplasty, facial feminization surgery, and hormone replacement therapy. In an interview, she credited transgender model Amanda Lepore and the death of her mother as sparks for her to go on with her transition – her mother passed away from leukemia in 2012. Gigi now resides in Los Angeles, California.
