Asal Kolaar Wiki, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography & More

Asal Kolaar is an Indian singer, lyricist, and rapper who works in Kollywood and Tollywood film industries. He is known for his Tamil music track titled ‘Jorthaale’ (2020).

Wiki/Biography

Vasantha Kumar P, popularly known as Asal Kolaar, was born in 1966 (age 26 years; as of 2022) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Family

Career

Lyricist

Reportedly, Asal collaborated with director Sathish Selvakumar for his Tamil language film ‘Bachelor’ in 2021 and worked as a lyricist. It was claimed that the song co-written by Asal received criticism because of sound design which became an advantage for him.

Poster of the film ‘Bachelor’

Rapper & Singer

Asal worked with Santhosh Narayanan, a singer and music director, on the song titled ‘Mustache Vestaache’ for the film ‘Parris Jeyaraj’ in 2021 following which he received the opportunity to appear in the music video titled “Pona Povura” of a Kollywood film ‘Mahaan.’

Asal’s work as a singer can be heard in the songs of many films including ‘Gulu Gulu’ and ‘Coffee With Kadhal.’ Kolaar and OfRo’s ‘Jorthaale’ went viral on social media.
