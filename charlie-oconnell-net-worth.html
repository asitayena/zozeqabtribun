Charlie O'Connell net worth and salary: Charlie O'Connell is an American actor and reality television personality who has a net worth of $600 thousand. Charlie O'Connell was born in New York City, New York in April 1975. He is the younger brother of actor Jerry O'Connell. He starred in season seven of the reality TV series The Bachelor in 2005. He ended up choosing nurse Sarah Brice. They dated for a period but were officially broken up by 2010.
