Black Coffee and Sarah Langa's rumours are growing after the two Mzansi celebs posted that they were in Miami around the same time.

The world-renowned producer and the gorgeous social media influencer fuelled rumours that they were indeed an item when they each posted that they were in Miami, US just an hour apart.

The Drive hitmaker was in Miami a few days ago and played at an epic party at LIV dance and night club. LIV also posted litty snaps of the Mzansi producer rubbing shoulders with the US A-listers who were at the party.

Sarah also took to Instagram and shared a snap of a beautiful view of South Beach Miami.

ZAlebs reports that Sarah has disputed the rumours but evidence proves otherwise. The publication alleged that this was not the first time that the two personalities were spotted in the same place. They were also allegedly spotted looking very cozy in Cape Town about a month ago.

In other entertainment news, Briefly News reported that Black Coffee's estranged wife Enhle Mbali has been loud and open about the horror she allegedly endured during her marriage.

The mother of two recently appeared in court after she accused her estranged husband Black Coffee of abuse. The matter was reserved by the court and a decision is still pending. Now Enhle is opening up about previous incidents where the DJ allegedly violated her rights. According to Enhle, the successful DJ abused her twice before the recent incident at her birthday party.

In a statement, Enhle revealed that she endured gaslighting, emotional and verbal abuse, physical abuse and financial abuse. She went on to say that the first incident of abuse occurred in 2014. She also accused the DJ of attempting to intimidate her using his financial muscle.

Enhle said she hoped that by speaking out, others would draw inspiration and learn from her experience.
