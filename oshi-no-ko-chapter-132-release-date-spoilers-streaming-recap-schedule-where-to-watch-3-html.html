Oshi No Ko Chapter 132 Release Date : Spoilers, Streaming, Recap, Schedule &amp; Where To Watch?

Journey into the Plot

The Manga, skillfully crafted by Aka Akasaka and beautifully illustrated by Mengo Yokoyari, follows Masaya and Gotanda on an enthralling journey. In the last chapter, Masaya emphasized the need to settle debts with Gotanda and proposed a daring plan to overcome financial challenges. The story’s consistency in captivating the audience promises more excitement in the upcoming chapter.

Oshi No Ko, a masterpiece by Doga Kobo, has achieved global acclaim with over 12 million copies sold by July 202The series, blending Drama, Mystery, and Supernatural elements, has been captivating audiences since its debut on April 23, 2020.
