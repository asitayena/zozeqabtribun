Sports Journalist, Mayanti Langer and her husband, Stuart Binny are blessed with one son

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, September 2, 2024

Asian beauty, Mayanti Langer is one of the popular names in the world of sport. She is a successful Indian sports journalist who is currently serving for the Star Sports network. Some of her works are Football Cafe on Zee Sports, 2011 Cricket World Cup, 2010 Commonwealth Games, 2019 Indian Premier League, 2010 FIFA World Cup with ESPN, and 2019 Cricket World Cup.

Indian journalist, Langer was born on 8th February 1985 in New Delhi, India. She was raised by retired army officer Lt. General. Sanjiv Langer and housewife mother alongside her siblings. Being a football craze from the beginning, she soon pursued her career as a sportsperson. While studying in the United States, her interest in football grew wider and wider and was also selected for college. 

Mayanti Langer life partner cricketer Stuart Binny 

Thirty-six years old, a sports journalist is a proud married woman. She exchanged her wedding vows with her longtime boyfriend, Stuart Binny. Stuart is a very popular name in the world of cricket. He is an Indian international cricketer who played Twenty20 international, One Day Internationals, and Tests for his country. He currently plays for Indian Premier League giants, Nagaland. 

Similarly, he has played for clubs like Hyderabad Heroes, Belagavi Panthers, Royal Challengers Bangalore, Mumbai Indians, Karnatak, and many more. Furthermore, he also holds the Indian record for best bowling spell in an ODI where he claimed six wickets for runs back in 2014 against Bangladesh. 

Mayanti Langer shares one daughter with her husband, Stuart Binny

Stuart and Mayanti are one of the power couples in the world of cricket. Being both involved in the same profession they have a huge amount of understanding among each other. According to the couple, they started dating one another in 2010. Having a similar goal and achievement in their life the pair soon got involved in a very serious relationship. In 2012 the beautiful sports couple accepted one another as husband and wife. 

The wedding ceremony was full of Indian cultures and dresses where numerous high-profile cricketers were seen there to greet the newly married couples. As of now, Mr. and Mrs. Binny are married for almost a decade and very much deep in love with one another. 

During this long nuptial span, they are blessed with one beautiful child son. They welcomed their child in September 2020 and shared their feeling in their respective social media account. Despite having a hectic schedule they are still taking good care of their child. 

Mayanti Langer's net worth

Mayanti has an impressive net worth of $400,000 as of 2021. She has accumulated all of her fortunes through her impressive career as a sports journalist. She has been part of various top-class sports events and her reporting style is loved and appreciated by almost every viewer. Similarly, she is one of the experienced female sports journalists in India, and with her deeds, she is paid a hefty amount of remuneration. 

Talking about her husband, Stuart's fortune, he is an extensive moneymaker. His net worth is around $3 Million as of 2021 which he accumulated through his impressive career as a cricketer. As a star player, he is also involved in various brands and endorsements. Another cricketer's wife, Lee Furlong has a net worth of $1 Million while her husband, Shane Watson has a net worth of $25 Million.
