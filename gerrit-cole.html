Gerrit Cole

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Birth Name: Gerrit Alan Cole

Place of Birth: Newport Beach, Orange, California, United States

Date of Birth: September 8, 1990

Ethnicity:
*father – Dutch
*mother – three quarters Italian, one quarter German

Gerrit Cole is an American professional baseball player. He is a pitcher for the New York Yankees of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Gerrit is the son of Sharon and Mark Cole. Gerrit is married to Amy Crawford, with whom he has a son. Amy is the sister of professional baseball player Brandon Crawford.

Gerrit’s paternal grandfather was Adrian Cole, Jr. (the son of Adrian Cole and Sarah Antonetta Van Der est). Gerrit’s grandfather Adrian was born in Michigan, to Dutch parents.

Gerrit’s paternal grandmother was Esther Plyman (the daughter of Gerrit/Gerritt Pijlman/Plyman and Meintje “Minnie” Meines). Esther was born in Iowa, to Dutch parents. Gerrit’s great-grandfather Gerrit was born in Vroomshoop, Twenterand Municipality, Overijssel, Netherlands, the son of John Pylman and Roelofje “Ruth” Baas. Meintje was born in Boksum, Menaldumadeel Municipality, Friesland.

Gerrit’s maternal grandfather was Charles James Heim (the son of Charles Lewis Heim and Agnes Marino). Gerrit’s great-grandfather Charles’s father was German. Agnes’ parents were Italian.

Gerrit’s maternal grandmother was Mary Lou Aversa (the daughter of Ralph Pasquale Aversa and Mary Ann Critelli). Gerrit’s grandmother Mary Lou’s parents were Italian, with her own mother having been born in Calabria.
