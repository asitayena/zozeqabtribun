Who Is Carissa Culiner Married To? Her Bio, Net Worth, E! News, Facts

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 23, 2024
Published Date: 24 Jun, 2020 Updated date: 24 Jun, 2020 By Phil

After getting married to her husband Shanon Culiner, Carissa Culiner welcomed a second child with her better half in April 2020. The wonderful news was circulated on E! News as the Culiner family got blessed with a baby girl.

Carissa Culiner is an American correspondent who rose to prominence after becoming the host and correspondent for E! News, and E! News Now. She has also made an appearance in the TV series called The Other Two in 2019.

Insights on Carissa Culiner Bio & Family: Birthday, Parents

The 34-years-of-age media personality was born as Carissa Renee Loethen in 1986, in Jefferson City, Missouri, the United States. As per wiki, she celebrates her birthday on February 21 every year. Carissa is among the three children of her parents, David Loethen and Diana Loethen. She has two older brothers named Kyle Loethen and Chad Loethen.

Speaking more about her family, Carissa’s father, David, is a co-owner of a wholesale amusement company, AQI. Similarly, her mother, Diana, works at a software company named ECI Solutions. Carissa’s brother, Chad, runs a Los Angeles based catering business which is named My Event LA.

Her Education Details

About 5 feet and 4 inches (1.63 meters) tall in height, Carissa attended the St. Joseph Cathedral School in Jefferson City for her education. She later enrolled at the Helias Catholic High School. Upon graduation, she got admission to Columbia College.

However, Carissa dropped out after a year and decided to relocate to Los Angeles. Eventually, she attended the Santa Monica Community College. She got homesick a year later and headed back to Jefferson City.

Carissa graduated in May 2008 with a BA degree in Communications from the University of Missouri.

Carissa Culiner’s Career, E! News, Salary & Net Worth

Carissa began her broadcasting career serving at the country radio where she co-hosted the show called Scotty and Carissa. Later in 2012, she became the managing editor and anchor at an online news company called Newsy.

Eventually, Carissa worked for Time Magazine and About.com. After that, she made the breakthrough in her career by becoming the host and correspondent for E! News, and E! News Now.  She hosted Live From E! from 2015-2019, E! News from 2015 to 2019, and Daily Pop from 2017 to 2020.

For her remarkable career as a correspondent, Carissa bags a healthy net worth that swirls around $15 million. Her annual salary from E! network still remains confined.

Married To Husband Shanon Culiner For 4 years

After dating for some time, Carissa walked down the aisle together with her husband, Shanon Culiner on 3 June 2016. It was about 1.5 years after Shanon proposed Carissa on 26 December 2014. The couple got engaged at Nobu, a famous sushi restaurant on the iconic beachside community of Malibu.

The pair got married in a lavish wedding ceremony held in Islamorada, Florida. The wedding was officiated by Shanon’s stepfather, Michael Josephs. The couple welcomed their first child, a son named Chase Culiner on 9 December 2017.

Carissa Welcomes Second Child With Her Husband!

Recently on 28 April 2020, Carissa welcomed her second child, a baby girl named Zoey Grace Culiner. The news was circulated on E! News. Speaking exclusively to them, Carissa said,

Delivery went extremely smoothly. I even got to do the Kourtney Kardashian thing and partly pull her out of me. It was the most amazing experience! We’re back home now, and mom and Zoey are doing great. Chase is loving being a big brother so far. He thinks Zoey is just the cutest thing!

It was back on 22 October 2019 when Carissa broke the news of her pregnancy to her companions at E! News. With the birth of her baby girl, the Culiner family is blessed with more joy.
